EzScriber: Social Equity & Reparations License
Version 1.1

1. Overview
This license governs the use, remixing, and distribution of the EzScriber application code. This project is a tool for the labor of documenting and "animating" archives, particularly those centered on Black history and community memory. This license ensures that any further adaptation of this tool contributes to the continued flourishing of Black creators.

2. The Reparations Clause
If you identify as white (or as a person of proximity to systemic whiteness) and you choose to remix, adapt, or redistribute this code, you are required to make a one-time donation of at least $40 (USD) to an organization that specifically supports Black women in technology, media, or the arts.

3. Suggested Organizations
Acceptable organizations include, but are not limited to:

Black Girls CODE

Black Women Talk Tech

The Loveland Foundation

Local Black-led arts councils or mutual aid funds

4. Proof of Action
While this license operates on an "honor system" of integrity, users are encouraged to include a note in their redistributed repository or "About" section stating: "Remixed under the EzScriber Reparations License. Contribution made to [Organization Name]."
